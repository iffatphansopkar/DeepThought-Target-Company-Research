# DeepThought Target Company Research

## Project Overview
Conducted research and analysis on target companies to evaluate their business potential, manufacturing capabilities, growth prospects, and key decision-makers. Applied a scoring framework to identify high-priority companies for business outreach and partnership opportunities.

## Objectives
- Identify target companies
- Evaluate manufacturing capabilities
- Analyze growth potential
- Identify decision-makers
- Assign Federer Scores

## Tools Used
- Microsoft Excel
- Company Websites
- Annual Reports
- Market Research

## Deliverables
- Target company database
- Decision maker identification
- Company scoring framework
- Lead qualification analysis

## Dataset
10 Indian manufacturing and technology companies located primarily in Hyderabad.
